Perso

I would say my essence hasn't changed much since I could walk. I still love to live. I love singing into the sky, painting form the heart, searching my own heart and sharing with those I care about. Above all, I want to test the boundaries of what we believe to be reality in this precious and fragile world we call Life On Earth. I believe without hesitation that we need to change the status quo and I want to find out how to do that. The journey has led me through a Ph.D. and academic philosophies, studies of traditional knowledge and perception, observation of the natural world and its inhabitants, and a myriad of experiences. I hope that along my travels, I have been of use to those around me, human and non-human. I continue on my search. Join me.
